⚔️ The Battle

Two wizards face each other across an enchanted coordinate plane, where Apples gleam at the integer lattice points — the sacred intersections where grid lines cross. Each turn, conjure a polynomial spell of the form y = f(x), tracing a magical curve through the plane. Every Apple whose coordinates satisfy your equation is consumed by your sorcery. The wizard who harvests the most apples claims victory.

⁂

🍎 Harvesting Apples

Your spell is a curve drawn on the coordinate grid. Any Apple that lies on the curve (at integer grid points) is consumed by your magic. The more apples your spell captures, the higher your score.

💡 A well-placed parabola can sweep entire rows of apples in one cast!
⁂

✍️ Casting a Spell

Type your equation in the input panel, then press Enter or click Cast. Every spell must be a true polynomial in x — a sum of powers, each bearing a whole-number coefficient:

f(x) = anxn + an−1xn−1 + … + a1x + a0

The Arcane Codex decrees four unbreakable laws — and one ancient freedom:

  • ⚡ Degree ≤ 10 — the highest power of x shall not exceed ten
  • ⚡ Integer coefficients only — every aᵢ must be a whole number; fractional sorcery (e.g. 2.5·x²) is forbidden by the ancient compact
  • ⚡ Bounded magnitude — |aᵢ| ≤ 10⁹; no coefficient may overflow the arcane registers
  • ⚡ No parentheses — grouping symbols are not permitted; expressions such as (1+2)·x or (x+1)(x³+1) are beyond the codex
  • ✨ Free ordering & repetition — terms may appear in any order (x^3*2 + x^8 is as valid as x^8 + 2x^3), and like terms may be repeated (x^8 + x^8 is permitted and combines by addition)
⁂

⚡ Game Modes

  • Free Play — All spells allowed. Perfect for learning the grid.
  • Balanced — Trivial spells (degree 0 & 1) are limited to 3 uses with an exponential cooldown (2s → 4s → 8s) after each cast. Master the higher arts!
  • Poly Masters — Only polynomial spells of degree ≥ 2 are permitted. True sorcerers only.
⁂

⏳ The Arcane Timer

Each turn is timed. If the hourglass runs out before you cast, your turn is forfeited. Plan wisely — hesitation is the enemy of victory.

⁂

🏆 Victory Conditions

The duel ends when all apples are harvested or time expires. Should the outcome remain unclear, the oracle resolves the duel through the following chain of judgement:

  1. 🥇 Score — the wizard with the higher accumulated score prevails
  2. 🍎 Apples eaten — if scores are equal, the one who consumed more apples wins
  3. ✍️ Fewer spells cast — if apple counts are equal, the more efficient caster (fewer expressions used) is crowned
  4. ⏱ Earlier last cast — if all else is equal, the wizard who finished casting sooner claims victory
  5. 🤝 Draw — if the fates decree it, honours are shared

May your polynomials be ever in your favour.
